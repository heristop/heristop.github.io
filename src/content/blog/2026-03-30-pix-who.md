I asked my Vibe Code Assist: "build me the most addictive game in the universe."

It said yes.

Obviously it always says yes.

So I had to get serious. Load the right SKILLS, use the right APIs, plan things out with "superpowers" like Neo in Matrix. A lot of iterations. And a lot of credits to generate the avatars, and I still don't know Kung-fu. 💸

## The result: Pix Who? 👨

24 suspects with degraded photos. Yes/no questions to eliminate. Sprays to earn to restore pixelated faces and reveal clues. 7 questions max. 2 wrong guesses and you're done. A daily challenge. Free. No signup.
